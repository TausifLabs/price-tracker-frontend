About This Project

In a market where precious metal prices fluctuate daily, making an informed decision about buying gold or silver can be challenging. This project was born from a desire to bring clarity and insight to consumers in Bangladesh. By providing a clear, historical record of price fluctuations, this tool empowers you to identify trends, compare different purity levels, and ultimately, choose the best time to make your purchase.

Beyond its practical use, this application also represents a personal journey into the world of full-stack web development, combining data scraping, backend API creation, and a dynamic frontend into a single, cohesive project.

How to Use

Master Controls

Use the Gold/Silver and Per Gram/Per Vori buttons at the top to set your primary view. The entire page, including colors, tables, and graphs, will instantly update to reflect your selection.

Interactive Price Table

The table shows the latest prices for all available purities. To dive deeper, simply click on any row (e.g., "18 Karat"). This action will immediately update the history graph below to show the price trend for that specific carat.

Historical Graph

The graph visualizes the price history for your selected carat. Use the dropdown menu above the graph to change the time period you want to analyze, from a week to six months.

Responsive Design

This application is designed to work beautifully on any device. Whether you're on a desktop or a mobile phone, the layout will adapt to provide a clean and intuitive experience.
